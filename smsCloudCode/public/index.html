Welcome to SwitchBoard! Bringing random text message pen-pals to Swat.

If you're a spec, you're automatically enrolled to group #rtt. Feel free to direct questions to Swatties at #swat.

SwitchBoard will automatically pair you up with a member in the campus group of your choice.

For example, you could message "#tango how do I cross-step?" to connect with a random tango-er. Or get some homework help with "#math how do I integrate by parts?". Message a random Swattie: "#swat sharples date?" and stop having lonely dinners by yourself. You get the picture!

Once you're chatting, you can text normally (without hashtags). You'll automatically stay connected as long as you're still talking. When you're done, just type #leave to end the conversation. Or just start a message with a new hashtag to begin chatting with another Swattie!

A few more things you can do:
  • #busy will make you unavailable to anybody on the service for 12 hours
  • #report will report your partner for abusive messages
  • #unsubscribe will unsubscribe you from the service (we'll miss you)
