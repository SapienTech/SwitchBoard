switchboard lets you text anonymously with anyone at Swat.

To start chatting, choose any group from the featured boards and text #groupname [your message]. You will be paired with an anonymous user from that group. Once the conversation has been initiated, you can text normally without hashtags. If 15 minutes pass without conversation, you and your partner will be disconnected automatically.

Join the groups you're interested in

To receive messages from other anonymous users, join any of the groups from the [featured boards] that you identify with. Other users may be paired with you when they send a message to your group.

Remember these useful hashtags

#leave: To leave a conversation, text #leave, or simply text another #groupname to automatically disconnect and join a new conversation.

#boards: To see the list of featured groups, text #boards.

#off: To stop receiving messages for any amount of time, text #off. When you're ready to start chatting again, text anything to the switchboard number and you will be reconnected.

#busy: To stop receiving messages for a 12-hour period, text #busy.

#unsubscribe: If you would like to disconnect from switchboard altogether (we'll miss you!), text #unsubscribe.

#help:(coming soon) If at any time you'd like to review this information, just text #help to receive a link to this page.

REPORTING: switchboard should be safe and fun for all users. If you receive an inappropriate or abusive message from a user, please text #report. Switchboard admin will be notified, and the reported user may be blocked from switchboard, according to admin discretion. Please note that under this temporary reporting system, the reported message and both user phone numbers are made available to switchboard admin. A future reporting system may use an anonymous three-strikes policy. User feedback or suggestions in this regard are much appreciated.

PRIVACY: switchboard has strong concerns for user rights and privacy. Please be aware, however, that while switchboard conversations are anonymous between users, switchboard is not a private chat platform. Because switchboard is in the beta-testing stage, information on user phone numbers, conversation text, times, and locations is automatically recorded in the admin database. This information may be used for bug-tracking and reporting, only, and will not be otherwise used, shared, or sold by switchboard.
